How it works

The base must resist tipping when the lateral force F acts at the load's CoM height h. The righting moment is (mload + mbase) · g · R; tipping moment is F · h. We solve for the smallest radius that achieves your safety factor, then nudge it larger or thicker based on the balance slider. Mass = π R² t ρ.

Estimates only — not a substitute for engineering review. Assumes rigid base on level ground, friction sufficient to prevent sliding.
